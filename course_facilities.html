Courses We Offer

We offer three best courses for student by Walchand College of Engineering

Diploma

Dive into a transformative learning experience with our Diploma in Engineering program. Designed to foster hands-on skills and theoretical knowledge, our program empowers students with a solid foundation in various engineering disciplines. Our dedicated faculty, curriculum, and state-of-the-art facilities ensure a dynamic and engaging educational journey.

BTech

Elevate your engineering aspirations with our B.Tech program, where innovation meets excellence. Our comprehensive curriculum, guided by seasoned faculty, equips students with cutting-edge skills in diverse engineering disciplines. Embrace hands-on learning, industry collaboration, and a nurturing academic environment.

MTech

Advance your expertise with our M.Tech in Engineering program, designed for aspiring engineering professionals seeking specialization and leadership roles. Engage in advanced research, collaborative projects, and specialized coursework guided by our distinguished faculty. Our commitment to innovation ensures a transformative learning environment.
